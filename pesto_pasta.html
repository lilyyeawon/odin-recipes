Pesto Pasta

Quick and easy pesto pasta with bright, bold flavor

Ingredients

  • Pesto
  • Pasta
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Toasted pine nuts

Recipes

  1. Blend up a batch of basil pesto
  2. Cook your pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water
  3. Before draining the pasta, reserve some of the starchy pasta water.
  4. Add 1/4 cup of this pasta water back into the pot with your pesto, and stir to thin the pesto.
  5. Toss in the cooked pasta, and mix until the pesto thoroughly coats it.
  6. Add a big handful of arugula and toss until it is barely wilted.
  7. Finish everything with a big squeeze of lemon, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and toasted pine nuts.
