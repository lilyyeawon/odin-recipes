Caprese Salad

A classic Caprese salad with soft, creamy fresh mozzarella, juicy seasonal tomatoes, and a generous amount of basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Basil
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Slice your tomatoes and mozzarella
  2. Layer them onto a platter with the fresh basil leaves
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper
