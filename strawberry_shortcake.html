Strawberry Shortcake

Bright, lemony cornmeal biscuits surround layers of fresh berries and cream

Ingredients
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Granulated sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Sea salt
  • Coconut oil
  • Lemon juice and zest
  • Eggs
  • Almond Milk
  • Whipped cream or coconut cream

Steps

  1. Whisk together the dry ingredients in a medium bowl.
  2. Add the coconut oil and use your hands to work it into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add the lemon zest, juice, almond milk, and eggs and mix until just combined.
  4. Turn it out onto a lightly floured piece of parchment paper, pat it into a rectangle, and fold it into thirds. Pat or roll the folded dough to about 1 inch thick, and freeze it for 20 minutes.
  5. After the dough chills, cut out the biscuits.
  6. Pop them in a 400-degree oven for about 17 minutes, or until theyâ€™re lightly golden brown around the edges.
  7. Pull the biscuits out of the oven and allow them to cool slightly before you assemble the shortcakes.
  8. Layer them with the strawberries and a dollop of whipped cream.
